What Happens On Save

Automatic record updates

  • The invoice row is saved into D1 with the VAT rate and computed totals.
  • The client is created or updated automatically from the contact details entered here.
  • The vehicle registration is attached to that client when vehicle details are provided.
  • The PDF is stored in the documents bucket with the invoice number in its filename.
