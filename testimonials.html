How We Work

Every installation follows the same disciplined path.

Whether the project is automotive, residential, or commercial, the process stays grounded in product clarity, careful preparation, clean execution, and practical client guidance.

Consultation & Film Selection

We start with the real use case: heat control, privacy, visibility, appearance, budget, and the environment the film needs to perform in every day.

  • Shade and product guidance based on actual priorities
  • Clear separation between entry, mid-tier, and premium films
  • Practical advice for cars, homes, offices, and glass-heavy spaces

Installation Day

Preparation, alignment, and finishing matter as much as the film itself. The install process is structured to protect the result from the start.

  1. Window inspection, cleaning, and surface preparation
  2. Controlled film handling and accurate positioning
  3. Clean edge finishing, final inspection, and client handover
