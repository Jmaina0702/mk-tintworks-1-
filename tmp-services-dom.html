FAQ

Questions clients ask before they book.

How long does window film last?

Quality residential and commercial films last 10–15 years or more with proper care. Premium films often exceed 20 years.

Will window film make my home or office too dark?

No. Modern films range from nearly clear to darker options, allowing the right balance of heat rejection, visibility, and privacy.

Can window film be applied to any type of glass?

Most films work on standard single-pane and double-pane windows. Special films are available for tempered, laminated, or low-E glass, which is why consultation matters.

Is chameleon tint legal in Kenya?

Yes for private vehicles when visibility requirements are respected. NTSA focuses on VLT percentages and prohibits highly reflective mirror films.

Will chameleon tint fade over time?

Quality chameleon films use UV-resistant top coats to protect against fading. With proper installation and care, the color-shifting effect should last for years.

Can I install tint myself?

DIY kits exist, but professional installation gives better adhesion, cleaner finishing, longer life, and proper warranty support.
